The Best Books According to Darius

My top 3 books of all-times

The Stormlight Archives - by Brandon Sanderson

The Way of Kings was my first introduction to The Stormlight Archives, and I have never looked back.The world is incredible the characters are extremely compelling, and complex while at the same time not being over done. The concept of the "Gods" and another world made up of concepts and "little gods" and their conection to the physical world is simply remarkable.

Mistborn series - by Brandon Sanderson

What I like most is how intricate the magic system is. Its almost as if it's a character in and of itself.The world building is phenomenal, and the characters equally so.

The Dark Tower - by Steven King

I like this series for its dark tone, and how it ties in the authors other works. I find the world settings very intresting, and each major conflict kept me on the edg of my seat. Of course there were some decisions I question, but thats why it's my third pick after all.
